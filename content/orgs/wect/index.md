We are a 501(c)(3) tax exempt nonprofit team providing communications services in large scale disasters and to charitable events within the Central Massachusetts area, mostly using amateur radio.

Our primary served agency is the City of Worcester Division of Emergency Management.  Additionally, we serve other agencies as needed and requested.

We also assist a variety of local charity organizations upon request with tactical communications during events such as marathons, triathlons, walkathons, or parades.

Our membership has diverse skills in that we are not all of one professional vocation. All of our volunteers operate our radio systems and are able to bring these skills and more to the task of assisting our served agencies when called on.
