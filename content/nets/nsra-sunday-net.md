The purpose of this net is:

- To serve as a weekly check-in and equipment test for fellow NSRA members, and all Amateur Radio operators within our coverage area. Signal reports will be given on request.
- To share news, events, and information, of interest to NSRA members and the local Amateur Radio community.

You may list any ham radio related items that you may have for sale, including the asking price.

The NSRA is a non-profit organization of approximately 150 members based mainly in the North
Shore area of Massachusetts. The club manages seven repeaters as well as an Echolink node;
NS1RA/R and IRLP Node #4427 on this repeater. More information about our repeater network is
available on the NSRA web site at <http://www.nsradio.org>.
