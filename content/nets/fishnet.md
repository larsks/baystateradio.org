A relaxed, local ham radio simplex net held every Thursday at 7:30 PM. We alternate between 6 meters (50.200 MHz USB) and 2 meters (146.500 MHz FM), giving everyone a chance to exercise their stations and have a friendly chat. The net is run by a primary net control station with the assistance of relay stations throughout the stations checked in. This will ensure we can have a larger audience participate.
