Many of the members of the Boston Amateur Radio Club meet to discuss club activities and other ham-related issues on the weekly BARC Net. Look for us on the Club’s VHF repeater on 145.23 MHz (-600, 88.5 input PL, 100 output PL) every Monday night at 9:00 p.m. local time!
