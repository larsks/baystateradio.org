Emmaus’ signature fundraising event, Cycle for Shelter, started in 1989 when our founding Executive Director completed a 1,200-mile bike ride from Georgia to Massachusetts, visiting more than 100 programs serving homeless and impoverished families along the way. Hundreds of Emmaus supporters and cyclists met him when he arrived in Haverhill.

Over the past 38 years, Cycle for Shelter has raised more than $2.4 million for Emmaus. It is an event the community looks forward to each year. Nearly 200 cyclists participate and dozens of local businesses and restaurants sponsor the ride.
