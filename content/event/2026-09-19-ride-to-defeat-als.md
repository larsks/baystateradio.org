The annual Ride to Defeat ALS: Massachusetts is an opportunity for friends, family, and the local community to join together to support those people affected by ALS. Funds raised at this event help to discover treatments and a cure for ALS, to serve, advocate for, and empower people affected by ALS to live their lives to the fullest. We offer multiple routes and ride lengths to participants of all abilities. Enjoy lunch, drinks and music at our post-Ride celebration!
