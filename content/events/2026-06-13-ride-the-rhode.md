Join us for Bike MS: Ride the Rhode, a one-day cycling adventure through the scenic beauty of Rhode Island. Whether you're rolling past iconic beaches, cruising down backroads lined with wildflowers, or coasting by centuries-old stone walls and historic homes, every mile immerses you in Rhode Island’s rich beauty—and brings us closer to a world free of MS.

Choose how you want to show up for others. We have volunteer options that range from pre-event assistance, to the start and finish line cheer section all the way to rest stop and safety support along the route — Bike MS would not be possible without volunteers like you.

See why people come back year after year to volunteer and change the lives of people affected by multiple sclerosis.
